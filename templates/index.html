Shaform

Shaform is a 1st year graduate student studying Computer Science and Information Engineering at National Taiwan University in Taipei City, Taiwan. Shaform enjoys Master the Mainframe as he is able to learn by doing, gaining experience from completing tasks on the mainframe. The competition inspires Shaform to accept new computing challenges. He is hoping the World Championship will help him improve and hone his coding and programming skills.

IBM Master the Mainframe World Championship

The IBM Master the Mainframe World Championship brings together the next generation of mainframe all-stars. Out of 20,000 IBM Master the Mainframe student contestants, 43 Master the Mainframe regional contest winners that have demonstrated superior programming skills on the mainframe have been selected to compete on the world stage.
